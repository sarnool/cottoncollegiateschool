Office of Development and Alumni Affairs

ODAA coordinates structured alumni affairs and development programs as an incorporated and transparent institutional body.

Role of ODAA

The Office of Development and Alumni Affairs coordinates alumni engagement within the ten-acre campus setting and manages social meetings alongside institutional development initiatives.

It operates as a unified incorporated body and follows local registration requirements under Act XXI of 1860.

Activities and Programs

The Association runs target-driven programmatic committees overseen by Executive Council members to support execution metrics across the state tier.

Affiliates and Partners

ODAA promotes structured collaborations that bridge state governance tracks, global technology entities, and the alumni database network.

Events and Programs Committee

Committee actions are managed by executive council teams. Registered members can coordinate program data through centralized mail channels.

Office of Development and Alumni Affairs

ODAA is the official wing of Cotton Collegiate Govt. H.S. School for alumni affairs and works alongside the association on the school campus.

Institutional role

The ODAA is the official wing of Cotton Collegiate Govt. H.S. School for alumni affairs. It is described in the source materials as an incorporated body working from the school campus to coordinate alumni engagement, social meet-ups, family gatherings, sports and cultural meets, and development-oriented programs.

The principal of the school serves as patron of the Alumni Association, and the Alumni Association maintains shared interests with ODAA while retaining the flexibility of a registered society under Act XXI of 1860.

Activities and programs

The association regularly runs dynamic target-driven programmatic committees overseen directly by respective Executive Council members to drive execution metrics across the state tier.

Affiliates and partners

Institutions thrive on structured collaboration. The association's development frameworks are intended to bridge the gap between state governance tracks, global tech entities, and the alumni database network.

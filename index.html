Legacy at a glance

Established in 1835, the school began with 58 students under Headmaster Mr. Singer and later became Gauhati Seminary before being raised to collegiate status in 1865, the first school in northeastern India to achieve that distinction.

The alumni association carries that legacy forward through mentorship, scholarships, chapters, and a broad network of professional and social support.

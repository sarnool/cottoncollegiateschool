Get Involved Through Membership

Membership is a simple, transparent, and voluntary way to reconnect with the institution that shaped your path.

Membership Benefits

Active members access premier corporate networks, deep analytical research library catalogs, direct validation options with ODAA, and specialty social and technology clusters.

Giving and Donations

Online and offline contribution tracks are available. While modern payment gateways and QR pipelines are being integrated, members may process contributions by bank demand drafts or checks payable to the Cotton Collegiate Alumni Association.

Why Participate

Engagement helps sustain a connected, high-value alumni ecosystem that supports both members and institutional development.

Vision, Mission, and Purpose

The Association works to connect members, share knowledge, and support social progress through coordinated alumni action.

Vision

To act as a catalyst to forge a close link between members and their representative establishments who seek to share the mission of the Institute and spread knowledge for the general good of the country and society at large.

Mission

The Association aims to bring members across the globe under one umbrella, share the available treasure of knowledge across fields, and work with its Alma Mater to foster institutional ideals for social benefit and growth.

Objectives and Activities

Capacity Building

Harvesting a pool of global talent to leverage developmental inputs systematically.

Catalytic Engagement

Guiding early engineering and science professionals through targeted organizational corporate platforms.

Promoting Excellence

Incentivizing instructional frameworks via localized merit awards and professional scholarships.

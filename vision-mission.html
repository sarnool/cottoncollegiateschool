Cotton Collegiate Govt. H.S. School

Vision and mission

The association exists to strengthen the bond between alumni and the alma mater and to use that network for the wider good of the school and society.

Vision

To act as a catalyst to forge a close link between its members and their representative establishments involved with the Institute who seek to share the mission of the Institute to achieve the objective of spreading knowledge to the general good of the country and the society at large.

Mission

The Association aims to bring its members spread across the globe under one umbrella, to share the vast treasure of knowledge available in various fields among them and to work in tandem with its Alma Mater to foster and permeate its ideals across, to the benefits of the society and join hands together for its growth.

Core aim and objectives

Core aim

Give back to the alma mater by building a single network of alumni members and maintaining a lifelong connection with the Institute.

Capacity building

Harvest a pool of global talent to systematically leverage developmental inputs.

Catalytic engagement

Guide early engineering and science professionals through targeted organizational corporate platforms.

Promoting excellence

Encourage instructional frameworks via localized merit awards and professional scholarships.

Social networking

Use alumni connections to encourage social and cultural activities that strengthen belonging with the alma mater.

Alumni chapters

Regional chapters extend the association beyond Guwahati and help sustain local membership and activity.

Regional role

The source materials describe regional alumni chapters in metropolitan cities as extended arms of the association. These chapters are encouraged to identify alumni, build local membership, create data banks, coordinate activities, and work in consultation with the Guwahati office.

How chapters help

  • Identify and connect alumni in each city
  • Build local membership and data banks
  • Coordinate chapter activities and gatherings
  • Work in consultation with the Guwahati office
